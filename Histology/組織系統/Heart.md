## Endocardium (內襯)
1. Endothelium
	- 特化成瓣膜 : 上下內皮，中結締
2. Subendocardium
	- 結締組織 + 平滑肌
	- **Purkinje fiber** : 特化心肌細胞，非神經，肝醣顆粒

## Pericardium
- fibrous pericardium (內有phrenic n.、pericardiacophrenic a./v.)
- serous pericardium (分parietal、visceral layer)

	- oblique sinus
	- transverse sinus : 胚胎**dorsal mesocardium開洞** (FC p.206)
